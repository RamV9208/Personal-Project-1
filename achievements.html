Achievements

Won the combat with Waif

Waif is a mysterious face-less human in Braavos. When I did not believe in the ideology and abilities of face-less people, the leader of the group ordered Waif to find and kill me. With the blessings of the seven, I was able to defeat Waif and show the power of a Stark. I wasn't sure if the face-less crew were impressed by my skills or they thought that I was the chosen one but I was accepted by the face-less people to provide me with training on being a face-less person, shapeshifter and above all a 'No one'

Erased House Frey

My brother Robb Stark loved a pretty women and chose to marry her. She is a health care provider and equally loved back my brother. On the other hand, house Frey were promised that my brother Robb will marry one of Frey's daughters in exchange for help in the past. This conflict existed but House Frey acted like they were supporting my brother's marriage but that night my whole family was massacre brutally. I took the revenge by wiping out the House Frey using my unparallelled skills.

Killied the night king

One thing all the westeros always feared and prepared to face was the long night. Beyond the wall, there was a king of the night called the night king with a highly powerful army of the dead. They are multi fold powerful than anyone else in Westeros. During the long night, when the night king with his army attacked Winterfell and when all the heros were out there fighting the army without much success, I skillfully caught a moment and killed the night king using a dagger made of Valyrian steel.
